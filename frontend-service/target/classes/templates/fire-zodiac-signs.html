Aries Zodiac Sign


  Element: Fire
  Color: Red
  Quality: Cardinal
  Day: Tuesday
  Ruler: Mars
  Greatest compatibility: Libra, Leo
  Lucky numbers: 1, 8, 17
  Dates: March 21 - April 19

Aries do what they want and do things their way. They are not afraid of conflicts, highly competitive, honest and direct. An Aries is not weighed down by the freedom of choice, and is perhaps the sign that is least conflicted about what they want. They throw themselves at the world eagerly and without fear. It is one of their most commendable qualities, but also what causes them a great deal of pain and grief. Aries are driven by a desire to prove themselves and their strength. They naturally take charge and are competitive and ambitious. Aries are spontaneous and courageous. They have a sense of adventure and love to explore. They are determined and bold, and are good at initiating new projects. They have high energy and can initiate quick actions. They can also be impatient, but are naturally active and do not like to waste time. While Aries like competition, they do not like to play games. They are highly self-aware, have strong opinions and are always ready to defend them.


Leo Zodiac Sign


  Element: Fire
  Color: Gold, Yellow, Orange
  Quality: Fixed
  Day: Sunday
  Ruler: Sun
  Greatest compatibility: Aquarius, Gemini
  Lucky numbers: 1, 3, 10, 19
  Dates: July 23 - August 22

Leos are bold, warm, and loving. They are also the ultimate showmen. They can dazzle with the theatrical flair of a Broadway star and the charisma of a politician. They are captivating personalities. They have a way with words, and can speak eloquently on just about any topic, no matter how quickly they have just been introduced to it. Leos inject ambition into everything they do. They are ruled by the heart. They are brash and confident and trust their instincts. Their actions are natural and effortless. Leos have an unrelenting appetite for growth that is easily satiated by the praise of others. When they are hurting, it shows. They will use their anger as a protective wall to shield their vulnerabilities. They want to be perceived as unbreakable because they are scared of admitting vulnerability, needing things from other people, being incomplete.


Sagittarius Zodiac Sign


  Element: Fire
  Color: Blue
  Quality: Mutable
  Day: Thursday
  Ruler: Jupiter
  Greatest compatibility: Aries, Gemini
  Lucky numbers: 3, 7, 9, 12, 21
  Dates: November 22 - December 21

Sagittarius is the ultimate empiricist. They will always choose principles over feelings and will often question who they are. They move from job to job, philosophy to philosophy, belief to belief. They are explorers of the human condition and are unafraid of change. Sagittarians feel like the world is their playground. They love to explore the unknown. They want to understand how the world works. On an unbounded quest to discover the whole of the universe inside of themselves, Sagittarians are unshackled from any particular worldly attachment. They are reckless in their pursuit of what they want, and often end up doing and experiencing things that defy conceptions of the possible. Sagittarius is an explorer. They are both the fearless adventurer and the jaded critic. They understand that knowledge comes in two forms: the shallow, disposable kind that comes from external sources, and the kind that comes from within. Sagittarius knows that external knowledge can be easily gained while internal knowledge is exponentially deeper and more powerful. They are on a quest to delve into the depths of the universe inside the human mind. They do not do this to prove that they have all the answers, but instead because they know that the journey is the destination.
