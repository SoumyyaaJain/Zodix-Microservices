Cancer Zodiac Sign


  Element: Water
  Color: White
  Quality: Cardinal
  Day: Monday, Thursday
  Ruler: Moon
  Greatest compatibility: Capricorn, Taurus
  Lucky numbers: 2, 3, 15, 20
  Dates: June 21 - July 22

The personality of a cancerous person is like wading chest deep in a lake of warm water. It feels sparkling and cool while it is touching the body, but you know that if you were to dive in, it would feel warm. The self-awareness of cancer is like the tides. They are constantly moving in and out of focus. Their personality is layered. They have many moods, some of which are contradictory, but they also have a deep, core self that persists. Cancers are weighed down by their own sorrows and the sorrows of those around them. They are frequently haunted by grief. It is hard for them to share their pain with others, and they are often afraid to be vulnerable because they carry a fear that people will use their weaknesses against them. Cancers have learned to hide their pain to avoid burdening anyone else. They pretend they are okay when they are not. Their emotions are like an exposed nerve. They can feel everything. They are like a tuning fork that vibrates at the slightest provocation. They tend to carry deep grudges because they cannot forget the emotional sting of a slight.


Pisces Zodiac Sign


  Element: Water
  Color: Mauve, Lilac, Purple, Violet, Sea green
  Quality: Mutable
  Day: Thursday
  Ruler: Neptune, Jupiter
  Greatest compatibility: Virgo, Taurus
  Lucky numbers: 3, 9, 12, 15, 18, 24
  Dates: February 19 - March 20

Describing a Pisces personality can be difficult because Pisces tend to evade distinction. Their behavior changes significantly based on who they are around. Pisces are just permeable membranes that pensively let things flow through them. They are cerebral sea sponges. They are boundless. They tend to dilute themselves with larger personalities to avoid having to form coherent identities. Most of the qualities usually associated with Pisces (dreaminess, emotionality, imagination) are internal processes that are difficult to observe from the outside. This is because Pisces are primarily inward-facing. They are not self-absorbed, but they are absorbed in themselves. Ultimately, a Pisces wants to dissolve. To wrest themselves of a physical form and diffuse into everything they touch as love. To exist in the gossamer liminal layer that hangs just barely above the material. To live their life like a romantic poem. To understand that reality and fantasy are neighbors on the same infinite plane.


Scorpio Zodiac Sign



  Element: Water
  Color: Scarlet, Red, Rust
  Quality: Fixed
  Day: Tuesday
  Ruler: Pluto, Mars
  Greatest compatibility: Taurus, Cancer
  Lucky numbers: 8, 11, 18, 22
  Dates: October 23 - November 21

The Scorpio personality is a profound chasm of infinite complexity (or at least how they project themselves). They are difficult people to get to know. They are psychological trap doors. They socialize from behind a double-sided mirror, always scanning, reading you while you can only see your own reflection. They prefer to be the people asking the questions. They remove your skin with their perceptive scalpel and take inventory of your pulsing viscera. They probe and push. They know the little things that make you tick. Your pressure points. The subtle ways to procure the answer they are seeking. They are keenly aware of power, its flows, and their position within its matrix. Despite their ability to be generally popular, there is something very lonely about Scorpios. They have a ruthless view of the world. Eat or be eaten. Every human interaction is a meeting of opposing powerful forces. A clashing of wants and needs where somebody wins and somebody loses. Their knowledge of others internal structures gives them an edge over the competition. They know how to play people against each other. They are forthcoming and evasive at the same time, depending on what is situationally beneficial. They are blunt, but not tactlessly so. They are intentional about the information they reveal. They are not liars, they do not espouse falsehoods, but they are masters of opening windows on certain truths while concealing others behind heavy curtains.
